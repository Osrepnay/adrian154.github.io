about

I'm Adrian. Due to an unfortunate accident, I have been sucked into a life of coding. I've made some pretty neat stuff on the downward spiral, though.

I've dabbled in all sorts of areas, from web development to language design as well as quite a bit of low-level tinkering. If you'd like to talk about programming, I would be more than welcome if you reached out.

You can contact me in a few ways. I would prefer if you messaged me on Discord at drain#5012, though if you would rather use the snailmail of the Internet you can also reach me at azhangcc@gmail.com. My network currently does not support IPoAC (IP over Avian Carriers), so any attempts to contact me via carrier pigeon will result in a connection timeout as per RFC 1149.
